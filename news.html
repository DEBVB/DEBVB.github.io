Recent News

2025 Season Tryout Dates Announced

Posted on August 10, 2025

Get ready to hit the court! Tryouts for the upcoming 2025 season will be held from March 3rd to March 5th in the main gym. All prospective players must have an updated physical form submitted to the athletic office before tryouts begin. We look forward to seeing everyone there!

Congratulations to Our Graduating Seniors!

Posted on June 5, 2025

A huge congratulations to the graduating class of 2025! We want to thank our senior players for their dedication, leadership, and hard work over the years. Your contributions to the program have been invaluable. We wish you the best of luck in all your future endeavors!

Parent & Player Pre-Season Meeting

Posted on February 15, 2025

A mandatory pre-season meeting for all interested players and their parents will be held on February 25th at 7:00 PM in the school cafeteria. We will discuss season expectations, schedules, and answer any questions you may have.
